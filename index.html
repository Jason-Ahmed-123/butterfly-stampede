Butterfly Stampede

Don't call them "bs"!

LA art-rockers debut album is out now, BUTTERFLY STAMPEDE #1, mixed and mastered by Grammy Award winning producer, Hugo Nicolson (Radiohead, Beck, Bjork).

The Butterfly Stampede band are: Jason Ahmed, British street thug-turned-musician, a rear admiral of sound, manning this Rocketship of Alternative Rock.

The Clash, Nirvana and REM were the inspirations that empowered Mexican guitarist Luis Lerma to escape his barrio and stampede into the world of music, just as a hoard of butterflies stampedes the oyamel fir trees of Michoacán.

Summer Carmichael, the Barry White of bass, a sophisticated soul and R&B musician, exuding love from every pore of his fret board while never forgetting the streets that taught him how to.

Don Kelly, choirboy and chamber singer, has entertained everyone from the Pope at the Vatican to the 21st Century Body-Rockers of Simi Valley. Kelly is the moral compass of this hodge-podge assemblage of working-class artists - the diverse personalities who comprise Butterfly Stampede.
